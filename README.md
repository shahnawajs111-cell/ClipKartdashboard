📊 ClipKart Sales Dashboard | Power BI
🚀 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the development of an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard for ClipKart using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The project involved data cleaning, transformation, KPI creation, DAX calculations, and dashboard design to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance across products, categories, and cities.

🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

Analyze overall sales performance.
Track key business KPIs.
Identify top-performing products and categories.
Evaluate city-wise sales contribution.
Understand revenue distribution across categories.
Support strategic decision-making through interactive visualizations.

Data Modeling
Created relationships between relevant fields.
Built calculated measures using DAX.
📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:

💰 Total Revenue

Measures the total revenue generated from all sales transactions.

📦 Total Orders

Tracks the total number of customer orders.

🛒 Total Quantity Sold

Measures the total quantity of products sold.

💵 Average Order Value (AOV)

Calculates average revenue generated per order.

🔥 Top Product Revenue

Identifies the highest revenue-generating product.

📉 Lowest Product Revenue

Highlights the product generating the least revenue.
